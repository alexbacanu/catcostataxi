# Welcome to CatCostaTaxi!

CatCostaTaxi is a website developed to help you estimate the cost of a taxi ride.

You can easily estimate the cost of a taxi ride in your city. All you have to do is enter your departure and destination locations, and we will show a transportation cost estimate for you.

We are aware that your time is very important, that's why we have developed a simple and intuitive interface that allows you to estimate the cost of a ride in just a few seconds. Also, our platform is user-friendly, so anyone can access our services. We are dedicated to constantly improving our website.

Thank you for choosing CatCostaTaxi to estimate the cost of your taxi rides!
